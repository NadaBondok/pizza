Welcome to Pino's Pizza, where your marble table awaits. Since 1965, when our founder, Nada Ahmed, opened the Damietta's first pizzeria in Damietta, Pino's Pizza has been bringing people together to enjoy delicious pizza with family and friends.

At Pino's Pizza, we aim to serve the highest quality products and provide uncompromising heartfelt services to our valued guests and community.
